\begin{abstract}
We include three works of different tastes in this thesis. In the first one, we speed up the approximate linear programming approach for network revenue management problem by solving an equivalent but simpler problem. In the second one, we study variant of facility location problem where clients have specific needs related to their types. The third one is quite pratical, we study how to reduce waiting time in appointment system by real-time resource sharing.
\end{abstract}
